# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and constructive.
* Assume good faith.
* Focus on ideas rather than individuals.
* Welcome newcomers and diverse perspectives.

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or discrimination.
* Personal attacks.
* Threats or intimidation.
* Publication of private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior violates this Code of Conduct.

## Contact

Questions or concerns may be directed to the project maintainers.
